About Me

Hi there! I'm Vinit, a Networking and Cloud Computing enthusiast with a sincere love for Python, Linux, and all things Cisco Networking. I like to troubleshoot and problem-solve, and I'm always curious to learn new things. When I'm not busy tinkering with networks and automating systems, you can find me hanging out with friends or diving into new topics to expand my knowledge.

I'm excited to showcase my impressive projects, including a media-sharing web app developed with AWS, containerizing an application with Docker and deploying it on AWS EC2 instances, and establishing communication between different SIP clients using the Asterisk Server. I'm a graduate of Humber College, where I earned Graduate Certificates in both Cloud Computing and Wireless Telecommunications. I also hold a Engineering Degree in Electrical from the Gandhinagar Institute of Technology.

In short, I'm a tech-enthusiast, a problem-solver, a trailblazer. I can't wait to collaborate with peers who share my passion, to showcase my talents and learn from theirs.
